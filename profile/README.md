## 🖌 Overview  
SpectraLayers Pro Advanced is an advanced spectral audio editing application from Steinberg that enables surgical manipulation of audio in the frequency domain, making it possible to remove specific sounds, isolate audio components, and repair recordings with precision that waveform editing simply cannot provide.

The spectral display renders audio as a visual frequency map where individual sounds appear as distinct patterns, enabling selection and removal of specific sounds — a cough in an audience, a phone ring, a microphone bump — by painting directly on their spectral signature.

AI-powered unmixing separates mixed audio into component stems including vocals, drums, bass, and other instruments using machine learning analysis, producing individual stem files from any stereo or multichannel mixed recording.

Harmonic selection tools identify and select the harmonic series of specific notes or instruments, enabling targeted removal or isolation of individual musical elements from recordings that share the spectral space with other sounds.

For audio restoration engineers, mastering engineers, and producers who need to manipulate audio at the frequency level with capabilities that go beyond conventional audio editors, SpectraLayers Pro Advanced is the spectral editing tool that operates where no other tool can reach.
